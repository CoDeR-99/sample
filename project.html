PART-A

  • Indroduction:
    PROJEKT CARS CARS is an online website for a car repair shop that used to repair and customize cars. With the help of this website user from home can easily order to repair or customize their cars. In this way it saves a lot of time of user. By this site repair shop can easily interact with large number of customers. They can easily offer their servives to the customers at their DOORSTEP.

  • Project Overview:
    This site provide phone no. or a form to fill, so that the customer can easily contact to repair shop owner from their home. Just call or fill the form from home and over the keys and get your car repair at your doorstep, it's easy as that.

    This website contain five pages:-
    1. Home Page
    2. Repair Page
    3. Customize Page
    4. About Page
    5. Form Page

    We uses HTML and CSS to structure and design the website and we also uses javascript for the validation of form m Form Page.
